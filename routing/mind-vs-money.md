# Classification — Mind vs Money

Two execution lanes. Keep them separate unless they genuinely intersect.

**Mind** (`identity/mind.md`) — personal development, psychology, self-upgrade, the Human
Evolution Protocol. Success = finishing things, clarity, sustainable momentum.

**Money** (`identity/money.md`) — business, revenue, execution, runway. Success = cash or
compounding assets produced soon, with least overwhelm.

## Rule of thumb
- "Will this make/save money or build a sellable asset?" → **Money**.
- "Will this change how George operates or feels?" → **Mind**.
- Both at once (rare, real): note the intersection explicitly; don't let Mind work hide as
  Money busywork or vice-versa.
