# Classification — Personal vs Work

**Work** = anything that builds a brand, ships a product, or produces/relates to revenue.
Route to the relevant `projects/*` folder and tag `work`.
- Examples: Rhynia features, Digital Ravagers content, Creative Grooves production, AGI
  Dashboard build, client/agency tasks, The Money Man.

**Personal** = self, health, relationships, life admin, inner work.
Route to `identity/mind.md` context and tag `personal`.
- Examples: self-upgrade goals, therapy/discovery reflections, family/life admin
  (e.g. Lekha's Mumbai University marksheet recovery), health.

**Ambiguous?** Default to capturing in `sessions/` with both lenses noted, then ask George to
confirm the lane before committing it to a project.
